# Sanity Check List

## 1. Market Price Comparison (Listed Companies)
- Deviation > 50%: Recommend re-verifying data/assumptions
- Deviation > 100%: Mandatory review of input data

## 2. WACC Range
- Normal: 6~15%. <5%: check rf/beta. >20%: check D/E, ERP.

## 3. Multiple Ranges
- EV/EBITDA 5~20x, P/E 8~30x, P/BV 0.5~5x

## 4. Cross-Validation
- SOTP vs DCF deviation >30%: review growth rate assumptions

## 5. Scenarios
- Probability sum = 100%, DLOM: unlisted 10~30% / listed 0~5%
- Growth rate > WACC is unrealistic
